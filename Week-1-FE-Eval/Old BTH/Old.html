                     Welcome

A throw back to New England days, Boston Towne Houses is located in Clinton Township and is one of the flagships of the affordable housing movement. Boston's HUD mortgage has been paid off and it is now a market rate cooperative without any restrictions on sale price or income levels, and share loans are available through National Cooperative Bank for people interested in purchasing a unit. Furthermore, all of its capital improvement programs have been completed. Carrying charges range from $260.00 to $338.00 per month with 1 bedroom ranches and 2-4 bedroom townhouse units.


Boston Towne Houses Cooperative is a proud member of:
Midwest Association of Housing Cooperatives
National Association of Housing Cooperatives
